Refund Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

1. Qualifying for a Refund

Our ₹499 Project Growth Audit fee is processed as a token of commitment. We offer a full 100% refund under the following conditions:

  • Unmet Criteria: If we review your submitted information and determine your company does not qualify for the audit, we will cancel the registration and issue a full refund immediately.
  • No Growth Potential: If during the audit call we determine there is no clear path to scale your project pipeline to ₹10 Crore, we will return your ₹499 fee in full.
  • Early Cancellation: You can request a refund if you notify us at least 24 hours prior to our scheduled Zoom audit session.

2. Non-Refundable Cases

We do not issue refunds if:

  • You do not attend the scheduled Zoom audit session without giving 24 hours prior notice.
  • You provide false or misleading operational statistics during registration.

3. Refund Processing

All approved refunds are credited back via the original payment route (Razorpay) within 5–7 business days.
